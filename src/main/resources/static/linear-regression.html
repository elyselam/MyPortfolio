What Predicts Yearly Amount Spent for Ecommerce Customers?¶

Below, I answer the question of how best to predict yearly amount spent on a fictional Ecommerce site. The principal predictors are the length of membership, time spent on website, time Spent on app, and average session length spent with a personal stylist. What to make of all this?¶

Clear from the coefficients of the linear model, the predictors length of membership and time on app have the biggest influence on yearly amount spent. If the company were to invest in either their website or app, I would suggest investing in the app to increase yearly amount spent on their platform.
